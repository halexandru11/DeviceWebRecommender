DeWR (Device Web Recommender) - Gimme App



1.Abstract


Gimme, also known as DeWR, is a web application that recommends electronic devices based on user's preferences (price, color, model, autonomy). A large variety of specific devices can be recommended (phones, TV's, PC's, headphones, laptops, smartwatches, cameras, tablets, keyboards, mouses).

Gimme uses scraping to get data from the most known national online retailers (Flanco, Emag, PC Garage, Altex, Media Galaxy).

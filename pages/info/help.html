Overview
Welcome to GIMME, the app that helps you find the electronic devices you want from a variety of online retailers. With GIMME, you can quickly search and filter products based on your preferences, compare prices and availability across different sites, and view detailed product information and reviews. Sign up now and start exploring all the features our app has to offer!
Sign Up
If you're new to GIMME, you'll need to create an account by following these steps:
  1. Click the Sign Up button on the home page or in the navigation menu.
  2. Enter your email address and create a password.
  3. Select the types of electronic devices you're interested in.
  4. Choose a color scheme that suits your style.
  5. Click the Sign Up button to create your account.
Sign In
If you already have an account, you can sign in by following these steps:
  1. Click the Sign In button on the home page or in the navigation menu.
  2. Enter your email address and password.
  3. Click the Sign In button to access your account.
If you've forgotten your password, you can reset it by clicking the Forgot Password link on the Sign In page and following the prompts.
Selecting a New Color Scheme
With GIMME, you can choose from a variety of color schemes to customize the look and feel of the app. Here's how to select a new color scheme:
  1. Open the Menu: Click the three horizontal lines icon on the top right of the app to open the menu.
  2. Click Themes: In the menu, click on the Themes option. This will take you to a page where you can view all available color schemes.
  3. Choose a Color Scheme: Browse through the available color schemes and click on the one you want to use. The app will automatically update to reflect your new color scheme.
  4. Enjoy your new color scheme: With your new color scheme selected, you can now enjoy using GIMME with a fresh new look!
That's it! With just a few clicks, you can select a new color scheme and customize the appearance of GIMME to your liking.
See Products
The Products page is where you can see a large list of electronic devices from multiple online retailers. Here's what you can expect to see on this page:
  • Product Cards: Each product is displayed on a card that shows the name of theproduct and its price. You can scroll through the list to view all available products.
  • Details Button: To see more information about a product, click on the Details button on the product card. This will take you to the Product Details page for that product.
  • Product Filters: You can filter the products shown on the Products page by clicking on the filter icon (looks like a funnel) and selecting the filters you want to use.
  • Save Products: You can save products you are interested in for later by adding them to your Whishlist.
  • Share: You have the ability to share products you find with your friends and followers on social media
That's it! With the Products page in GIMME, you can easily find and compare electronic devices from multiple online retailers.
Using Filters
With GIMME, you can quickly and easily filter the products you see to find exactly what you're looking for. Here's how to use filters in GIMME:
  1. Access the Filters Page: To access the filters page, click on the filter icon (looks like a funnel) on the Productspage.
  2. Choose Your Filters: In the filters page, you can select the following filters:
    • Sites: Select the online retailers you want to search from.
    • Type: Choose the type of electronic device you want to search for (tablet, phone, laptop, etc.)
    • More Filters: Refine your search even further by setting filters for price range, autonomy range, color, and model.
  3. Apply Filters: Once you've selected your filters, click the Apply Filters button to see your filtered results.
  4. Remove Filters: If you want to remove a filter, simply click on the X icon on the filter capsule on the Products page.
That's it! With GIMME's easy-to-use filtering system, you can quickly find the products you're looking for across multiple online retailers.
